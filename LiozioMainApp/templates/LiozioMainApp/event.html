Celebrating Excellence at Liozio Companies' First Quarterly Awards Ceremony

Friday, November 15, 2024, marked a momentous occasion for Liozio Companies as the team came together to celebrate their inaugural Quarterly Awards Ceremony. Hosted by the charismatic Sheila Akpaso, the event was a vibrant testament to the company’s commitment to recognizing excellence and fostering a culture of achievement.

A Night to Remember
The atmosphere was electrifying as employees, management, and special guests gathered to honor the outstanding efforts of individuals who have gone above and beyond in their roles. The event highlighted the values that define Liozio Companies: innovation, teamwork, and an unwavering dedication to success.

A Word from Leadership

In his keynote address, the CEO of Liozio Companies praised the awardees and all employees for their dedication, stating, “Our people are the cornerstone of our success. Today, we celebrate not just individual achievements but the collective strength that drives Liozio Companies forward.”

  • Date of Event : November 15th, 2024
  • Published by : Desmond Ucha
  • Venue : JOOMBOW 1, Bodija-Ashi, Ibadan
  • Host : Sheila Akpaso
  • Category : LC Quarterly Awards Ceremony
  • Notable Invitees : Harlem Solicitors
  • Awards : Learning Legend Award - Sheila Akpaso
    Team Player Award - Desmond Ucha
    Most Creative Employee - Victor Ajileye
    Special Recognition Award - Oladele Alabi
    The Best Employee of the Quarter - Sheila Akpaso

The Star Awards and Their Recipients
The evening was punctuated with exciting award presentations. Each recipient exemplified the spirit of Liozio Companies, setting the bar for future achievements.

Learning Legend Award : Sheila Akpaso – Recognized for her relentless pursuit of knowledge and professional development.

Team Player Award : Desmond Ucha – Honored for his collaborative spirit and exceptional support for colleagues.

Most Creative Employee : Victor Ajileye – Celebrated for his groundbreaking ideas and innovative contributions.

Special Recognition Award : Oladele Alabi – Acknowledged for his extraordinary dedication and impact on key projects.

The Best Employee of the Quarter : Sheila Akpaso – A well-deserved second accolade of the night for her unparalleled commitment and performance.

Looking Forward
As the event drew to a close, attendees reflected on the accomplishments celebrated and the milestones yet to come. The ceremony not only reinforced a sense of pride among the staff but also set the stage for future endeavors and achievements.
